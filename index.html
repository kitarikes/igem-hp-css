・Rebirth as Yellow Phosphorus

We learned how the current method of phosphorus recovery, the MAP method, works because it is expensive to maintain the equipment and the price of recycled phosphorus is more expensive than that obtained from phosphorus ore. Therefore, we began to think about reusing it as yellow phosphorus, which is relatively expensive.

・The State of Malnutrition

We've learned that Japan is facing more of a problem of poor nutrition than eutrophication. Due to improvements in Japan's sewage environment, the amount of phosphorus released into the environment is decreasing. Some organizations want to release a certain amount of phosphorus to produce good, seafood.
